Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you book.

Timing & Access

Most events run 2pm to 6pm. If a different finish time applies, it is shown on the event page.

Doors open at the stated start time (usually 2pm). The show runs straight through with no scheduled breaks.

No published last entry. You can arrive whenever suits you after doors open.

Re-entry is at staff discretion.

Tickets

Tickets usually start from £10. Typical range is £10 to £15, with Eventbrite fees added at checkout. Group tickets available for 4+.

Tickets are non-refundable unless an event is cancelled. See the Eventbrite listing for transfer options.

If not sold out, a limited number may be available on the door. Card or cash accepted.

The Vibe

Adults. Age guidance is 18+ recommended unless stated otherwise on the event page.

Whatever makes you feel fabulous. Sequins, trainers, jeans and a nice top. There's no strict dress code. Comfortable footwear encouraged because you will be dancing.

Flashing lights are used (no strobe). Confetti cannons appear at most events.

Official photographers capture highlight moments. If you prefer not to be photographed, speak to the photographer or a team member on the day.

Venue & Practical

Accessibility varies by venue. Limited seating areas are available. Many venues have accessible toilets. Contact hello@boomevents.co.uk or speak to the venue directly for specific access needs.

Challenge 25 is in operation. Small bags only; all bags may be searched. Backpacks are discouraged.

Bars are available at all events. Some venues offer food. Free tap water is always available at the bar.

Contact the venue directly for lost property after the event.

Contact

Email hello@boomevents.co.uk. We aim to reply within 48 hours, Monday to Friday.

Still have questions? Just ask.
