Hen Party Ideas Northampton: 2026 Guide

The best hen do activities in Northampton, from daytime discos to cocktail classes.

Planning a hen party in Northampton? Whether you're the bride, the maid of honour, or the friend who always ends up organising everything, finding the right hen do activity can be a headache. You need something everyone will actually enjoy, that fits the budget, and that doesn't require a three-hour train journey.

Here are the best hen party ideas in Northampton for 2026, starting with the one we think you'll love most.

1. THE 2PM CLUB: Silent Disco Day Party (Our Top Pick)

A four-hour silent disco party running every Saturday from 2pm to 6pm at The Picturedrome, Northampton. Three channels of music through wireless headphones, so everyone picks their own vibe: 80s and 90s, 00s anthems, or sing-along classics. Full club production with professional sound and lighting. Real event, real venue, real photos to remember it by.

  • When: Saturdays, 2pm to 6pm
  • Price: From around £10 per person
  • Group bookings: Available for parties of all sizes
  • Home by 7pm: Sunday completely sorted

This isn't a gimmick. THE 2PM CLUB has been running events across the Midlands for over 12 years. Real photos from real events, not AI-generated stock images. Check the gallery and see for yourself.

2. Cocktail Making Classes

Several venues in Northampton town centre offer cocktail making workshops where you learn to mix a handful of drinks with a professional bartender. Sessions typically run 1-2 hours and work well for groups of 8-20. A solid option if the bride-to-be loves her espresso martinis.

3. Afternoon Tea

The classic hen party afternoon option. Several hotels and cafes in and around Northampton serve afternoon tea with prosecco upgrades. It's relaxed, photogenic, and suits groups where not everyone drinks. Book well in advance for weekends.

4. Spa Days

A few spa venues within easy reach of Northampton offer group packages with treatments, pool access and lunch. Good for smaller groups, though it can get pricey once you add up individual treatments. Best booked months in advance, especially for Saturdays.

5. Dance Classes

From burlesque to street dance, there are private group dance class options available in Northampton. Sessions usually last about an hour and work best as a warm-up activity before heading on to something else. Great for groups who want to learn a routine together.

6. Escape Rooms

Northampton has a few escape room venues that can accommodate hen parties across multiple rooms. Sessions run about an hour. A good shout if the group likes a challenge, though not everyone's idea of a party. Works best paired with drinks or dinner afterwards.

7. Life Drawing

Hen party life drawing sessions are available through mobile providers who come to your chosen venue in Northampton. Typically 2 hours, with a model and all materials provided. Guaranteed laughs, especially when the results come out. Works well for groups of 10-20.

Why a Daytime Disco is the Perfect Hen Party

Still weighing up the options? Here's why a daytime disco keeps winning the group chat vote for hen parties in Northampton:

  • Everyone can make it. 2pm on a Saturday is the one time slot that doesn't cause babysitter drama, shift-swap negotiations, or the classic "I can't do late nights anymore" dropout. The plan that actually happens.
  • No hangover, no Sunday writeoff. Home by 7pm, still buzzing, remembering every moment. Compare that to piecing together the night from blurry photos and a sense of dread the next morning.
  • The group chat actually agrees on this one. It's not too wild for the quiet ones, not too tame for the party crowd. Three music channels on the headphones means everyone picks what they want to hear.
  • Proper photos to remember it by. Daylight plus professional event lighting means photos you'll actually want to share. Not the 2am flash-face nightclub shots nobody shows their mum.
  • From £10 per person. For a group of 10, that's £100 for a full four-hour event. A fraction of what a night out costs, and the bride actually remembers it.
