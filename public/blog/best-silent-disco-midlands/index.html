What Is a Silent Disco?

A silent disco is an event where music is delivered through wireless headphones instead of a traditional speaker system. Every person in the room wears a set of headphones and chooses which DJ channel to listen to. The result is a room full of people dancing, singing along, and having the time of their lives, all while controlling their own audio experience. It is one of the fastest-growing event formats in the UK, and for good reason: it works brilliantly for groups where everyone has different musical tastes.

The 3-Channel Wireless Headphone Experience

When you arrive at a 2PM CLUB event, you collect a pair of wireless silent disco headphones. Each set has a button to switch between 3 DJ channels, each playing a different style of music. The headphones light up in different colours depending on which channel you are listening to, so you can see who is on your wavelength across the room.

You control the volume. You choose the channel. If your friend wants to listen to something different, they can. Then you both end up back on the same channel for the big singalong moments. It is the best of both worlds: personal choice and collective energy.
