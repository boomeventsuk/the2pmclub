Questions People Ask Before They Book

WHAT TIME DOES IT START AND FINISH?
Doors open at 2:00pm and the party runs until 6:00pm. You'll be home by 7pm-ish with your whole evening ahead of you!
WHAT MUSIC WILL BE PLAYED?
Wall-to-wall 80s, 90s and 00s anthems - the songs everyone knows and loves. Think Madonna, Oasis, Britney, ABBA, Whitney Houston, and everything in between. If you know every word, we're playing it!
IS THERE AN AGE LIMIT?
Yes, our events are for over-25s. We want to create the perfect atmosphere where everyone can let loose and enjoy themselves without feeling out of place.
WHAT SHOULD I WEAR?
Smart-casual works perfectly. Come comfortable and ready to dance! Think jeans and a nice top, or whatever makes you feel great on the dancefloor.
CAN I BUY TICKETS ON THE DOOR?
Our events often sell out in advance, so we strongly recommend booking online to guarantee entry. Any remaining tickets may be available on the door, subject to capacity.
IS THERE A BAR?
Yes! The venue has a fully licensed bar serving a range of drinks. You can enjoy your favourites while dancing to the classics.
