The Venue: Mattioli Woods Welford Road Stadium

This is a big one. Welford Road Stadium, home of Leicester Tigers, is one of the most iconic sporting venues in the East Midlands. For THE 2PM CLUB launch, we're taking over a dedicated events space within the stadium grounds with capacity for up to 1,000 people. Stadium screens add a visual spectacle you won't find at a standard club night, and the production values are being scaled up to match the occasion. The venue is well-connected by road and public transport, with dedicated parking available on site. It's a setting that matches the ambition: this isn't just a party, it's a launch.

Address: Aylestone Road, Leicester, LE2 7TR
