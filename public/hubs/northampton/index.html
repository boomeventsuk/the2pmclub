Daytime Disco in Northampton

Where it all started. Night-out energy in the afternoon, home by 7.

Northampton is home turf. This is where THE 2PM CLUB kicked off, and over a year of sell-outs later, the town knows the drill. From the iconic Picturedrome on Kettering Road to the brand new summer dates at Franklin's Gardens, Northampton has taken Saturday afternoons and made them legendary. Confetti cannons, singalongs, and the kind of energy you thought you had to stay out until 2am to find. Except here, you're home by 7, Sunday feels brilliant, and the group chat is already planning the next one. The get-together that actually happens, every single time.

The Venues

The Picturedrome is a Northampton institution. Intimate enough to feel the bass in your chest, big enough to lose yourself in the crowd. It's where THE 2PM CLUB found its feet, and the atmosphere on event days is something else entirely. Located on Kettering Road, it's easy to reach from across the town.

Cinch Stadium at Franklin's Gardens takes things outdoors for summer 2026. Home of Northampton Saints, this is a proper upgrade for the warmer months. More space, bigger production, same energy. If you've been to a Picturedrome show, imagine that with sunshine.
