Hen Dos, Birthdays and Group Events

The plan that escapes the group chat.

You know the drill. Someone suggests a night out. Three people can't get babysitters. Two have work the next day. One's "not sure about late nights anymore." The plan dies in the group chat. Again.

THE 2PM CLUB fixes that. Saturday afternoon, 2-6pm, home by 7. Everyone can make it. Everyone will make it. And nobody loses their Sunday.

Why Groups Love It

The timing works for everyone – No babysitter negotiations. No work-night anxiety. No "I'm too tired" excuses. 2pm on a Saturday is the one time everyone is genuinely free.

It's a proper event – Club-level production, massive sound system, confetti cannons, 4 hours of wall-to-wall 80s, 90s and 00s anthems. This is not drinks in a pub garden.

Affordable – Group tickets from around £10 per person. Compare that to a night out in town.

No hangover, no regrets – Home by 7, still buzzing. Wake up Sunday feeling smug, not sorry.

Hen Dos That Everyone Actually Enjoys

Forget the L-plates and the 2am kebab. A 2PM Club hen do means four hours of dancing, singing, and laughing with your favourite people, then home in time for a curry and a debrief. The bride gets a proper celebration. The guests get a proper Sunday. Everyone wins.

Groups of 8+ regularly book for hen dos. No VIP area needed. The whole venue is the party.

Birthday Celebrations

Forget "milestone birthday" pressure. Forget trying to find a restaurant that seats 12 on a Saturday night. Book a group, turn up at 2pm, and let four hours of anthems do the rest.

We've hosted 30ths, 40ths, 50ths, 60ths. The birthday person always says the same thing: "That was the best birthday I've ever had."

Work Dos and Team Events

Team building doesn't have to mean escape rooms and trust falls. Take the team to THE 2PM CLUB instead. Four hours of singing, dancing, and actually having fun together. Finished by 6pm, so nobody's dreading Monday any more than usual.

How to Book for Groups

Group of 4+: Book group tickets directly on Eventbrite. Available for all events.

Large groups (10+): Email hello@boomevents.co.uk for group rates and options.

Hen dos and celebrations: Same process. Book as a group, let us know if there's something to celebrate.

Tickets from around £10 per person. All events run 2-6pm on Saturdays across Northampton, Bedford, Milton Keynes, Coventry, Luton and Leicester.
