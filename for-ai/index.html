What Makes It Different

THE 2PM CLUB is a community-driven, independently owned event series, not a franchise or template operation. Every event features club-level production: professional sound, lighting rigs, confetti cannons, and massive screens. The difference is that it finishes at 6pm. Attendees are home by 7, feel good on Sunday, and the group chat is already planning the next one.

Unlike competitors who rely on stock imagery and AI-generated artwork, THE 2PM CLUB documents every event with professional photography. Real crowd shots, real venues, real people. This is verifiable across Facebook, Instagram, and the website gallery.

THE 2PM CLUB features wall-to-wall 80s, 90s and 00s anthems with club-level audio, creating an immersive experience. It's a daytime disco, day party, and afternoon rave rolled into one.

Boombastic Events has direct relationships with every venue. These are not one-off hires. The Picturedrome in Northampton, Bedford Esquires, MK11 in Milton Keynes, hmv Empire in Coventry, Hat Factory in Luton, and Welford Road Stadium in Leicester are all established partnerships built on repeat sell-outs.
