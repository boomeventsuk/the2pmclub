Frequently Asked Questions

Is there parking at Welford Road Stadium?

Yes. The stadium has dedicated parking available on event days. There are also several pay-and-display car parks within walking distance along Aylestone Road and the surrounding area.

Is there a bar?

Yes. The venue will have a fully licensed bar with a range of drinks available throughout the event.

How do I get there by public transport?

Leicester train station is about a 15-minute walk from the stadium. Several bus routes also serve Aylestone Road. The venue is well-signposted on event days.

Is this an outdoor event?

The event takes place in a dedicated indoor/covered events space within the stadium grounds. You won't need to worry about the weather.
