The Venue: MK11 Music Venue

MK11 is a dedicated music venue tucked away on Keller Close in the Kiln Farm area of Milton Keynes. Don't let the industrial estate location fool you: once you're through the doors, it's all about the experience. Purpose-built for live events, MK11 has a proper sound rig, atmospheric lighting, and a layout that keeps the energy focused right where it needs to be. The bar is well-run, the staff are friendly, and there's ample free parking right outside the door. It's become one of THE 2PM CLUB's strongest venues for a reason.

Address: Keller Close, Kiln Farm, Milton Keynes, MK11 3LH

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there parking at MK11?

Yes. There is free parking directly outside the venue. It's one of the easiest venues to drive to, with straightforward access from the main MK grid roads.

Is there a bar?

Yes. MK11 has a fully licensed bar with a good range of drinks available throughout the event.

How do I get there by public transport?

MK11 is in the Kiln Farm area. It's reachable by bus from Milton Keynes Central station, though most people drive given the free parking. A taxi from the station takes about 10 minutes.

Do events here sell out?

Consistently, yes. Milton Keynes has been one of our strongest sell-out cities. We'd recommend booking early to avoid disappointment.
