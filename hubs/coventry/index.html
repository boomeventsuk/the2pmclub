The Venue: hmv Empire

hmv Empire is a landmark Coventry venue on Hertford Street, right in the city centre. With a capacity of 800, it's a serious room with a serious sound system. The venue has hosted some of the biggest names in live music, and on a 2PM CLUB afternoon, all of that production quality is pointed squarely at giving you the best four hours of your week. The main floor is spacious enough for a proper crowd while still feeling connected, and the bar is well-stocked and accessible throughout. Getting there couldn't be easier, with Coventry train station a ten-minute walk away and plenty of city centre parking nearby.

Address: 22 Hertford Street, Coventry, CV1 1LF

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I park for hmv Empire?

There are several city centre car parks within a short walk, including West Orchards and Cathedral Lanes. The venue is also a 10-minute walk from Coventry train station.

Is there a bar?

Yes. hmv Empire has a fully licensed bar with a wide selection of drinks available throughout the event.

What time do doors open?

Doors open at 2pm and the event finishes at 6pm. We recommend arriving close to doors for the full experience.

Is the venue accessible?

hmv Empire has step-free access to the main floor. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please get in touch before the event and we'll make sure you're looked after.
