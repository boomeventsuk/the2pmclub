The Venue: Bedford Esquires

Bedford Esquires has been at the heart of Bedford's live music and events scene for years. Tucked away on Bromham Road, it's the kind of place with proper character: exposed brick, a sound system that fills every corner, and a layout that puts you right in the middle of the action. The bar runs the length of the room, so you're never far from a drink, and the stage area gives every show a front-row feel. It's intimate without being cramped, loud without being overwhelming, and it has that rare quality of making every event feel like a proper occasion.

Address: 60A Bromham Road, Bedford, MK40 2QG

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I park for Bedford Esquires?

There is street parking on Bromham Road and nearby side streets. The Riverside car park is a short walk away and offers plenty of spaces on Saturdays.

Is there a bar?

Yes. Bedford Esquires has a fully licensed bar with a good range of drinks available throughout the event.

What time do doors open?

Doors open at 2pm and the event runs until 6pm. Get there early to make the most of it.

Will tickets sell out?

They have done before, yes. Bedford dates move quickly, so we wouldn't recommend leaving it to the last minute.
