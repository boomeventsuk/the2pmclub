The Venues

The Picturedrome is a Northampton institution. Intimate enough to feel the bass in your chest, big enough to lose yourself in the crowd. It's where THE 2PM CLUB found its feet, and the atmosphere on event days is something else entirely. Located on Kettering Road, it's easy to reach from across the town.

Cinch Stadium at Franklin's Gardens takes things outdoors for summer 2026. Home of Northampton Saints, this is a proper upgrade for the warmer months. More space, bigger production, same energy. If you've been to a Picturedrome show, imagine that with sunshine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there parking at The Picturedrome?

There is street parking on Kettering Road and surrounding streets. The Mounts car park is a short walk away. We recommend arriving a little early on event days as spaces fill up quickly.

Is there a bar at the venue?

Yes, both The Picturedrome and Franklin's Gardens have fully licensed bars. Drinks are available throughout the event. There is no need to bring your own.

What time do doors open?

Doors open at 2pm and the event runs until 6pm. We recommend arriving close to doors to make the most of it.

How do I get to Franklin's Gardens?

Franklin's Gardens is on Weedon Road, just west of the town centre. There is dedicated parking on site. It's also served by local bus routes from the town centre.
