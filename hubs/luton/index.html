The Venue: Hat Factory Arts Centre

The Hat Factory is one of Luton's most distinctive venues. A converted hat factory (the clue's in the name) on Bute Street, it's been reimagined as a creative arts space with a proper events room that's ideal for a 2PM CLUB afternoon. The sound is excellent, the space has real character, and its central location makes it easy to reach from anywhere in town. The bar serves a solid selection throughout, and the staff know how to run an event. It's intimate enough to feel the energy from every corner of the room, which is exactly what you want when the confetti drops.

Address: 65-67 Bute Street, Luton, LU1 2EY

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I park for the Hat Factory?

The Hat Factory is in Luton town centre. The Galaxy Centre multi-storey car park is a 5-minute walk away, and there is also on-street parking on surrounding roads.

Is there a bar?

Yes. The Hat Factory has a fully licensed bar open throughout the event.

Can I get there by train?

Yes. Luton station is about a 10-minute walk from the venue. It's on the Thameslink line, making it easy to reach from London, Bedford, and surrounding areas.

Will it sell out again?

The debut sold out with demand far exceeding capacity. We'd strongly recommend booking early. The get-together that actually happens only works if you've got a ticket.
